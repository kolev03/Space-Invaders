HOW TO PLAY THE GAME:

The core gameplay is faithful to the classic **Space Invaders**. Up to 10 waves of alien hordes spawn at the top of the screen and move downward, shifting direction each time they reach the edge. You have four blockers positioned between you and the aliens, each with 16 HP. Your goal is to eliminate the invaders before they reach your position.

Special Actions:

Every 7th alien you destroy with your basic attack will drop a power-up. The drop can either be a shield or a gamma ray. The shield protects you from incoming damage for a limited time, while the gamma ray unleashes a powerful beam that destroys everything in its path â€” including blockers. You also have a difficulty selection. EASY: 3 lifes, slow aliens, slow alien missiles. HARD: 1 life, fast aliens and fast alien missiles.
